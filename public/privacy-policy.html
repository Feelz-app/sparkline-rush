Privacy Policy

Sparkline Rush

Effective date: May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sparkline Rush handles information when you use the game. Sparkline Rush is a mobile arcade game with local score tracking, cosmetic unlocks, chests, optional rewarded ads, and banner ads.

Information We Store On Your Device

Sparkline Rush stores gameplay progress on your device so the game can remember your personal records, sparks, unlocked skins, cosmetics, chests, boosts, shields, extra lives, daily progress, and settings. This gameplay information is stored locally on your device and is not sent to our own server by the current launch version of the game.

Advertising

Sparkline Rush uses Google AdMob to show ads, including rewarded ads and banner ads. Google and its partners may collect or receive information from your device to provide ads, limit fraud, measure ad performance, and improve ad delivery. This may include device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app activity, ad interactions, diagnostics, and approximate location inferred from network information.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Optional Rewarded Ads

Rewarded ads are optional. If you choose to watch a rewarded ad, the game may grant an in-game reward such as a revive, chest, sparks, or boost after the ad finishes. These rewards have no real-money cash value.

Children

Sparkline Rush is not directed to children under 13. The game is intended for a general teen-and-up audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through our own servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. AdMob and Google advertising partners may process data as described in Google's privacy policies and advertising disclosures.

Your Choices

  • You can use the in-game Privacy Options button where available to manage ad consent choices.
  • You can reset or delete your device advertising ID in your Android settings.
  • You can clear local game progress by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards for the app experience, but no app, device, or internet service can guarantee complete security.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when Sparkline Rush changes, such as adding online leaderboards, accounts, purchases, or new advertising features. The effective date above will be updated when changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the developer contact email listed on the Sparkline Rush Google Play Store listing.
